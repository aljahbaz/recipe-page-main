Simple Omelette Recipe

An easy and quick dish, perfect for any meal. This classic omelette combines beaten eggs cooked to perfection, optionally filled with your choice of cheese, vegetables, or meats.

Preparation time

  • Total: Approximately 10 minutes
  • Preparation: 5 minutes
  • Cooking: 5 minutes

Ingredients

  • 2-3 large eggs
  • Salt, to taste
  • Pepper, to taste
  • 1 tablespoon of butter or oil
  • Optional fillings: cheese, diced vegetables, cooked meats, herbs

Instructions

  1. Beat the eggs: In a bowl, beat the eggs with a pinch of salt and pepper until they are well mixed.
  2. Heat the pan: Place a non-stick frying pan over medium heat and add butter or oil.
  3. Cook the omelette: Pour in the eggs and tilt the pan to evenly spread them.
  4. Add fillings: Add your chosen fillings over one half of the omelette when itâ€™s partially set.
  5. Fold and serve: Fold the omelette, let it cook for another minute, then serve.
  6. Enjoy: Serve hot, optionally with extra seasoning.

Nutrition

Per serving (without optional fillings):

  • Calories: 277 kcal
  • Carbs: 0 g
  • Protein: 20 g
  • Fat: 22 g
